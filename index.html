About Us


Welcome to Voice of Light Christian Network

Voice of light Christian Network (VLCN) was concieved through an open vision (trance) on 25th August, 2018 when God spoke to His son Apostle Bola Ishola where he showed him an object that looks like a very big transformer, He said "I have deposited my engine of fire and light into you, therefore go into every part of the world with this light and liberate my Children from every form of darkness, raising unto me a generation that will serve me".

VISION STATEMENT

Transforming the afflicted and repositioning the lost into light by the help of Holy Spirit through the Sacrifice of the SON (Jesus).

MISSION STATEMENT

Taking the Light of the SON (Jesus) and the endowment of the Spirit of God to the nations of the world to cause a great changewith the purpose of raising generations of men and women that will serve the Lord.

Founder

Apostle Bola Ishola is a preacher and the founder of Voice of Light Christian Network (VLCN), he is also the president of Bola Ishola Foundation (BIF). He studied Plant Breeding at Federal University Of Agriculture, Abeokuta Ogun State Nigeria. As expected many pressured him to start ministry during and after his university days that he can't be this anointed and not have a base, he totally refuse to follow the voice of men and rather wait patiently on the Lord for years until the Lord bring him into an open vision (TRANCE on 25th August, 2018) that birth Voice of Light Christian Network, praise God.
